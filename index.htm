Each day, Bajoink features an unbeatable deal on great stuff to do, see, eat, or buy in Tacoma. By guaranteeing businesses a minimum number of customers, we get group discounts you won't find anywhere else.

We sure are. We're passionate about local communities and getting people out and about doing cool things. You get a great deal, our businesses get great customers and we get to make it all happen.
